# Bibliography Curation Note

## Scope

The source library contained 95 candidate PDFs across the EV, social and digital innovation, and endogenous innovation folders. Three EV-industry items were excluded during curation.

## Deduplication

- Candidate PDFs after exclusions: **92**
- Unique files after SHA-256 deduplication: **81**
- Unique publications represented by the local source PDFs after bibliographic deduplication: **79**
- Final bibliography after incorporating additional verified records: **83 unique records**

Exact file duplicates were identified by SHA-256. Bibliographic duplicates were consolidated using DOI, canonical title, authorship, publication year, and document-version evidence.

## Metadata Verification

| Category | Count |
|----------|------:|
| Final bibliographic records | 83 |
| Records with verified DOI | 79 |
| Records without DOI but with a stable official identifier or repository record | 4 |
| Hosted third-party PDFs | 1 |
| Records represented by citation and source link only | 82 |

Metadata were reconstructed from the uploaded PDFs and checked against DOI records, publisher metadata, scholarly indexes, stable repository records, and the documents' own title pages. RDF/XML fragments, PDF-producer strings, false years derived from metadata objects, and truncated file-name titles were discarded.

No DOI was inferred from an incomplete string. Records without a DOI are identified through stable JSTOR, ISBN, or MPRA records.

## Hosting Policy

One article is hosted because its CC BY 4.0 licence explicitly permits redistribution. All other records are represented through DOI or stable-source information.
