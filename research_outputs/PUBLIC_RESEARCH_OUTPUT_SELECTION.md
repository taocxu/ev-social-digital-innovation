## Selection Principles

1. Author-finalised versions only
2. PDF preferred as the canonical public format
3. DOCX included only when verified free of track changes, comments, hidden text
4. No external copyrighted materials
5. No intermediate drafts or tracked-changes copies
6. No process notes or internal management records

## Excluded Materials

- DOCX files with detected track changes
- Historical and intermediate drafts
- Co-author revision copies and annotated versions
- Research notes, literature notes, and reference lists
- External academic papers, policy documents
- Award application and management records
- Presentation artwork
- Process notes and working documents
- All inventories, classification maps, logs, and audit trails
